ZUPPA TUSCANA

Yield: 4 servings

  • 1 lb Italian Sausage
  • 2 large (or more) Russet baking potatoes (slice in half, then into 1/4" slides)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/2 can Oscar Meyer real bacon bits
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups Kale or sill chard, chopped
  • 2 cans chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  1. Cook sausage in a 300 degree Farenheit oven for 30 minutes. Drain on paper towel and cut into slides.
  2. Place onions, potatoes, chicken broth, water and garlic in pot. Cook over medium heat until potatoes are done.
  3. Add sausage, bacon, kale, salt & pepper to taste, and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  4. Add whipping cr eam, heat through and serve.
